about neomotiveparts

NeoMotiveParts is a plastic injection molding company focused on supplying high-quality components for automotive and industrial applications. Founded in January 2025, our goal is to combine strong engineering support, reliable manufacturing, and export-ready logistics to serve OEMs, Tier suppliers, and aftermarket brands.

We specialize in engineered plastic parts such as brackets, HVAC and airflow components, interior trims, and custom housings. With a strong focus on design-for-manufacturability and process control, we help customers turn ideas into stable, repeatable production parts.

company history

from launch to growth

2025 – NeoMotiveParts is founded in January 2025, with a focus on plastic injection molding for automotive and industrial components. Initial investments are made into tooling, quality systems, and customer development.

2025 – First projects are launched with key customers, including aftermarket and restoration brands, focusing on brackets, trim parts, and functional plastic components.

2026 – Planned expansion of capacity and continued development of export-oriented programs, with additional tooling and extended product ranges for multiple vehicle platforms.

2027 and beyond – Roadmap includes additional molding capacity, more complex tooling, and wider coverage across automotive, EV, and industrial applications.
