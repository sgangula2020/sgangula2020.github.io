Machinery

NeoMotiveParts operates modern, high-precision injection molding machines designed for both prototype and high-volume production. Our equipment is carefully maintained and calibrated to deliver consistent, repeatable parts for demanding applications such as automotive and industrial products.

Tooling & Molds

We collaborate with experienced toolmakers to design and build molds that maximize part quality and minimize cycle time. From new tool development to mold modification and maintenance, our focus is on durability, efficiency, and tight dimensional control across every production run.

Design & Engineering

Our engineering team supports you from concept to production with DFM (Design for Manufacturability), material selection, gating and cooling strategy, and part optimization. We help reduce weight, improve performance, and lower total cost without compromising reliability.

Production & Quality

Every batch is produced under strict process controls, supported by real-time monitoring and documented work instructions. Our quality team performs dimensional verification, visual checks, and functional testing to ensure your parts meet specification—every time.

Why NeoMotiveParts?

End-to-End Injection Molding Support

From initial design reviews and tooling development to molding, assembly, and packaging, NeoMotiveParts provides a complete solution. Our team works closely with OEMs and Tier suppliers to reduce launch risk, shorten lead times, and bring high-quality plastic components to market faster.


Consistent Quality for Demanding Applications

We understand that critical components must perform flawlessly in the field. By combining robust process controls, trained operators, and detailed inspection plans, we deliver consistent, repeatable parts suitable for automotive, EV, and industrial environments where reliability is essential.
