Injection Molding Services

End-to-end plastic component manufacturing

At NeoMotiveParts, we provide complete plastic injection molding solutions – from early design support and tooling all the way to validated, production-ready parts. Our presses and processes are optimized for automotive and industrial components where consistency, durability, and dimensional accuracy are critical.

We support prototype, low-volume, and high-volume runs, helping customers launch new programs faster while maintaining stable processes, reliable lead times, and competitive piece costs.

How we work on every project

Every new part follows a structured approach that reduces risk and improves quality from day one. Our engineering and production teams collaborate closely with your designers and buyers to ensure a smooth launch.

  • Part review & DFM recommendations
  • Tool build, trials, and optimization
  • PPAP, validation, and stable production

Secondary Operations & Assembly

We can integrate multiple steps after molding, including basic trimming, fastening, sub-assembly, labeling, and packaging. By combining these operations at NeoMotiveParts, you reduce handling, freight, and complexity in your supply chain.

Quality, Documentation & Logistics

Our team supports dimensional reports, capability studies, and full PPAP documentation where required. We also help coordinate packing standards, export documentation, and shipping so your components arrive ready to use – whether your plant is local or overseas.
