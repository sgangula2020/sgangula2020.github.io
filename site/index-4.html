Our projects & customers


Aftermarket plastic components for leading brands

NeoMotiveParts partners with customers like Jeff, Brancos Graveyard, Auto Crafters, and NPD to develop and supply injection molded plastic components for restoration and aftermarket programs.

Our team supports each project from early design discussions and DFM reviews to tooling, trials, and stable serial production. Parts are engineered for fit, finish, and durability, so they install correctly and perform reliably in real vehicle environments.

For these customers, we’ve helped:
  • Convert metal brackets and small hardware into lighter plastic components.
  • Develop interior and exterior plastic trim suited for classic and modern platforms.
  • Align tooling, packaging, and logistics to support export and warehouse distribution.
Each project is managed with clear timing, transparent communication, and documented quality so our customers can focus on growing their catalogs and programs.
