Injection molded components for modern vehicles

Purpose-built plastic parts for OEMs and Tier suppliers

NeoMotiveParts specializes in engineered plastic components designed for automotive and industrial applications. Our products are developed to meet specific performance, packaging, and durability requirements – from interior touch points to under-hood brackets and EV subsystems.

We work with a wide range of materials, including PP, ABS, PC/ABS, PA/PA-GF and other engineering plastics, allowing us to balance weight, cost, thermal resistance, and mechanical strength based on your program needs.

Designed around function, fit, and manufacturability

Every NeoMotiveParts product begins with a clear understanding of how it will be used in the vehicle and how it will be assembled at your plant. We design around:

  • Precise interfaces, clips, and mounting features
  • Thermal and chemical environment in real use
  • Efficient molding and stable long-term production

Key product categories

HVAC & Airflow Plastics

Ducts, housings, vanes, and doors designed to support consistent airflow, low noise, and robust assembly. Components can be developed for both ICE and EV platforms with tight dimensional control.

Interior Trims & Functional Parts

Interior bezels, covers, small panels, and functional brackets that must look good and assemble smoothly. We focus on surface quality, consistent color, and robust clip design.

Brackets, Clips & Mounting Features

Lightweight plastic brackets and clips that replace metal, reduce weight, and simplify assembly. Designed for strength, repeatable engagement, and long-term durability in vibration and temperature.

Under-hood & Powertrain Parts

Engine bay and powertrain-adjacent plastics where temperature, fluids, and packaging are challenging. Material choices and design are tuned to each application.

Applications & customization

Tailored to each platform and region

Platform-specific solutions

We support multiple vehicle segments – from passenger cars and SUVs to commercial vehicles and EV platforms. Parts can be adapted for different markets, regulatory requirements, and customer-specific standards.

From concept to catalog part

Whether you are launching a new program or looking to localize an existing component, NeoMotiveParts can help with design reviews, tooling, validation, and ongoing production. Many components can also be standardized and used across platforms to reduce complexity and cost.

Interested in a custom part?

Share your CAD data, key requirements, and volumes, and our team will recommend a manufacturing approach, material, and tooling concept. Together, we can convert your requirements into a stable, repeatable injection molded product.
